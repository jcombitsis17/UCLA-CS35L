There are 5 main subwindows.

Top Left: Shows names/messages of all commits, as well as a tree structure
on the left showing the branch structure and including mergepoints.

Top Middle: Shows names of the author of each commit, along with his/her email.

Top Right: Shows date and time of each commit.

Bottom Left: Shows the ChangeLog for the commit. At the top of the ChangeLog, 
there are links to jump to parent/child commits.

Bottom Right: Shows a list of any/all files that were changed in the commit.
