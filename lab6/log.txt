single-precision floating point, -N to specify number of bytes, and 64M
because a float is 4 bytes, so we needed to specify 4 * (16 million). The sed
command was used to get rid of the octal counter in the left column of the 
output. tr command was used to eliminate white space. Finally sed '1d' deletes
the first blank line.
